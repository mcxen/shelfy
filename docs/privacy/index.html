Privacy Policy

The app

Shelfy is a desktop application that runs entirely on your computer. No file names, no file contents, and no usage data ever leave your device. All data — including your sorting rules, action history, and settings — is stored in a local SQLite database on your hard drive.

The website

This website (shelfy.cc) uses Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use the site. We only load Google Analytics after you explicitly consent via the cookie banner.

Google Analytics may collect:

  • IP address (anonymized where possible)
  • Device and browser information
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referrer and approximate location

This data is processed by Google and stored on their servers. You can reject analytics cookies at any time — the site works perfectly without them.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties
  • We do not use advertising or tracking pixels
  • We do not embed social media widgets that track you
  • We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar tools

Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, or restrict processing of your personal data. Since we do not collect personal data, these rights are technically not applicable — but we support the spirit of the regulation.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, open an issue on GitHub or reach out via Ko-fi.
